# BiomeClaw Executive Summary

## Live Demo

- Public demo URL: [https://tzeroagent.online](https://tzeroagent.online)
- Current demo channel: Telegram
- Product name: **BiomeClaw**
- Positioning: a private AI health companion that remembers context, maintains continuity, and supports behavior change through conversational coaching

## One-Line Pitch

BiomeClaw is a private, always-available health companion that helps a user stay consistent with sleep, training, routines, and recovery through a conversational experience that feels personal, remembers what matters, and runs on dedicated infrastructure.

## Why This Matters

Most health products are either:

- content libraries that do not adapt to the person
- trackers that collect data but do not help at the right moment
- chatbots that feel generic, forgetful, or unsafe for sensitive personal context

BiomeClaw is designed to solve the gap between data collection and real human follow-through. The product is built around continuity. It should remember the user, understand what time it is for them, respond with a grounded tone, and help them take the next useful step instead of generating generic wellness talk.

## What Makes It Different

- **Continuity by design**: the assistant keeps a durable view of goals, friction points, routines, and open loops rather than resetting every session.
- **Personal timing**: responses adapt to local time, quiet hours, morning context, and conversation gaps.
- **Private architecture**: each user gets an isolated workspace and runtime context, reducing cross-user contamination risk.
- **Action-oriented coaching**: the assistant is optimized for one clear next step, not long motivational monologues.
- **Voice-ready**: voice notes can be transcribed and handled as normal conversation input for more natural updates.
- **Proactive, but controlled**: reminders and nudges are constrained, contextual, and suppressible, not spammy.

## Current Demo Scope

The current stakeholder demo is intentionally focused and stable.

- Telegram is the primary user surface.
- Hosted onboarding and setup are available through the web flow.
- The public demo is deployed at [tzeroagent.online](https://tzeroagent.online).
- The environment has been reset as a fresh demo environment with no legacy users.
- WhatsApp is out of scope for this demo.

## Demo Story To Present

The strongest stakeholder narrative is not "AI chat." It is "a continuity engine for health behavior."

Suggested framing:

1. A user lands on the onboarding flow and connects Telegram.
2. The user starts talking naturally instead of filling out a sterile form.
3. BiomeClaw remembers the user's goals, preferences, and recent struggles.
4. The assistant adjusts how it speaks based on the user's local time and current context.
5. The user can send either text or a voice note update.
6. The assistant replies calmly, briefly, and with a practical next step.
7. Over time, the assistant can follow up on open loops and support consistency.

## Ideal First Customer Value

BiomeClaw is strongest where people need ongoing support between appointments, classes, or coaching sessions.

Strong early-fit customer profiles:

- a coach or health professional who wants lightweight between-session continuity
- a wellness brand that wants a premium retention layer instead of a generic chatbot
- a behavior-change product that needs a more human conversational layer
- a founder-led health service that wants a differentiated AI companion experience

## Business Value

- Improves retention by creating continuity, not just content consumption.
- Increases perceived personalization without requiring constant human staff time.
- Creates a premium product surface that can justify higher pricing or concierge tiers.
- Opens a path to channel-based engagement where users already are, starting with Telegram.
- Builds a reusable interaction layer that can later support broader care, coaching, or wellness workflows.

## Why A First Customer Should Care Now

- The product is already live in a hosted demo environment.
- The onboarding-to-chat flow is demonstrable end to end.
- The current version is optimized for trust, stability, and usefulness rather than novelty.
- A first customer can help shape the highest-value workflow before the product broadens.

## Current Constraints

This demo is a strong product-direction proof point, but it should be described honestly.

- It is not a medical device.
- It is not a diagnostic or prescribing system.
- The current public demo is optimized for Telegram.
- The proactive layer is intentionally lightweight and conservative.
- The right next step after stakeholder validation is a focused pilot, not broad-scale launch.

## Recommended Ask To The Stakeholder

The cleanest ask is a structured pilot conversation.

- Review the live demo and the product brief.
- Validate whether the continuity and coaching model fits your users.
- Identify one narrow pilot use case.
- Define success around engagement, user trust, and repeated use.

## Short Closing

BiomeClaw is not trying to be a generic chatbot for health. It is being built as a personal health companion that can remember, respond with context, and create momentum in everyday behavior change. The current demo at [tzeroagent.online](https://tzeroagent.online) is the first concrete proof of that direction.
